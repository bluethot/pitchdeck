Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted name in Nigerian real estate

A Legacy of Trust Since 2012

PrimeProperties was founded in 2012 by Chinedu Okonkwo, a visionary entrepreneur who saw the need for transparent and reliable real estate services in Nigeria. Starting with just three employees and a single office in Victoria Island, we've grown into one of Lagos's most trusted property developers.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to make property ownership accessible, secure, and hassle-free for every Nigerian. We understood the challenges faced by property buyers - from dubious land titles to complicated payment processes - and set out to change the narrative.

Today, we've successfully delivered over 50 projects, helped 500+ families own their dream homes, and expanded our operations across three states. But our core values remain the same: integrity, transparency, and customer satisfaction.
